Lasagna

Just Like Grandma Used to Make

An Italian classic that will delight your tastebuds. Layered with deliciously seasoned beef and melted mozzarella cheese, this lasagna is our most nostalgic recipe yet. Try it for yourself!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspood dried basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced

Instructions

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, oinion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water.
  3. Season with sugar, parsley, basil, salt, and Italian seasoning.
